### SCENE 1.2 — THE ARRIVAL

*10:00. The main elevator chimes—a different sound than usual. Deeper. Older.*

*The doors open.*

*THREE FIGURES step onto the Intake Floor.*

*The first is tall and thin, dressed in a suit that seems to absorb light. Their face is difficult to focus on—features that slide away from memory even while looking directly at them. A name badge reads: AUDITOR PRIMARY.*

*The second is shorter, broader, dressed identically. Their movements are precise, mechanical, as if each gesture has been calculated in advance. Name badge: AUDITOR SECONDARY.*

*The third—*

*KAEL freezes.*

*The third Auditor is familiar. The face. The posture. The way they hold their head slightly tilted, as if always listening for something others can't hear.*

*MIRA.*

*But not Mira. The features are Mira's, but the eyes are wrong. Too old. Too deep. The expression is Mira's professional neutrality, but underneath it—nothing. No recognition. No humanity.*

*Name badge: AUDITOR TERTIARY.*

*KAEL INTERNAL (all tones):*
> *Mira. She's... she's one of them now. The Archivist said the Auditors incorporate souls who get too close. This is what they meant.*
